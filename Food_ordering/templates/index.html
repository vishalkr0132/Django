South Indian food

South Indian cuisine is known for its diverse flavors, aromatic spices, and extensive use of rice, lentils, and coconut. It is a cuisine that is popular not just in South India but throughout the country and around the world.

Burger

Fast food is a type of food that is prepared and served quickly, often through a drive-thru or takeout service. It typically includes foods that are high in calories, fat, salt, and sugar and are not considered to be healthy in large quantities..

Chicken Tandoori

Chicken tandoori is a popular Indian dish made by marinating chicken in a mixture of yogurt and spices, and then roasting it in a tandoor, which is a traditional clay oven. The marinade typically includes ingredients such as ginger, garlic, cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala, which give the chicken its distinctive flavor and aroma..
